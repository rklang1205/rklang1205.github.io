You get up from the floor, and the whispers from the wall get more frantic, until someone clearing their throat silences the bunch. You move closer to the door, anxious as to what you'll find on the other side.

"So, you're finally awake. Took you long enough," a deep male voice muttered from the other side of the metal door. He sounded uninterested in what was happening, and relatively annoyed that it took you so long to wake up. You couldn't make out too many of his facial features, except for his paler skin and dark eyes as they glared at you through the slit in the door.

"Before you continue to waste my time, Let me explain what you are doing here. We are... scientists, developing a new sort of biochemical invention. Your genetic makeup is the ideal combination to lead to a successful result, and I am really, really sick of volunteers leading to failed attempts. So we just, kind of... you know, took you. All in the name of science, right? So basically, cooperate with us and you might live, or else we literally cannot let you go and you'll be trapped here forever. What do you say?"

"NO."

"I'm in."
